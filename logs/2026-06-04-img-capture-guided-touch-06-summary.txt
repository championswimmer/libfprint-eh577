That hash is still exactly equal to the standalone idle post-init frame hash and still differs from both successful standalone finger-hold frame hashes.

## Conclusion

A small delay experiment is not enough to move EH577 runtime behavior off the idle zero-frame path.
The next useful branch is either:

1. a stronger pacing experiment,
2. or `usbmon` comparison if stronger pacing also leaves the runtime stuck on the idle-identical payload.
