## Interpretation

This strongly suggests the remaining issue is not transport or basic timing-helper failure.
Instead, the runtime path is still polling the wrong capture mode for EH577.

Earlier standalone probe evidence showed:

- meaningful EH577 data appears on the **post-init** path
- the **repeat** path stayed zero-filled even during finger hold

The libfprint runtime was still switching into repeat polling after the first post-init frame, which is likely why guided touch 02 still saw only zero frames.

## Next fix chosen

Patch the driver to keep EH577 on **post-init polling** instead of switching into the repeat-only loop after the first `64 14 ec` frame.
